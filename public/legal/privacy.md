# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

CantonLink ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our decentralized finance aggregator service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Wallet Address:** Your public blockchain wallet address when you connect
- **Transaction Data:** On-chain transaction details (publicly visible on blockchains)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies:** Session tokens, preferences, analytics

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Private Keys:** We never access or store your private keys
- **Personal Identity:** We do not require name, email, or KYC
- **Custodial Access:** We never control your funds

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve the Service
- Process transactions and quotes
- Analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
- Detect fraud and prevent abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Third-Party Services

CantonLink integrates with third-party services:

### 4.1 Aggregators & Protocols
- **ParaSwap, Jupiter, LI.FI, 1inch:** We share your transaction parameters with these services to fetch quotes and execute trades. Each has its own privacy policy.

### 4.2 Analytics
- **Google Analytics:** We may use analytics to understand user behavior (anonymized data)

### 4.3 RPC Providers
- **Blockchain RPCs:** Your wallet connects to blockchain nodes to read/write data

### 4.4 WalletConnect
- **Wallet Connection:** WalletConnect facilitates secure wallet connections

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We may share information with:
- **Service Providers:** Third-party aggregators to execute trades
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law or legal process
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger or acquisition

## 6. Blockchain Transparency

All on-chain transactions are **publicly visible** on blockchain explorers. Your wallet address and transaction history are permanently recorded on public ledgers.

## 7. Cookies

We use cookies for:
- Session management
- User preferences
- Analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may limit functionality.

## 8. Data Retention

- **On-Chain Data:** Permanent (blockchain immutability)
- **Off-Chain Data:** Retained as long as necessary for service provision
- **Analytics:** Anonymized, retained for up to 2 years

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:
- Access your data
- Request deletion (off-chain data only)
- Opt-out of analytics
- Data portability

**Note:** On-chain data cannot be deleted due to blockchain immutability.

## 10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption
- Secure API endpoints
- Non-custodial architecture
- Regular security audits

**However, no system is 100% secure. Use at your own risk.**

## 11. Children's Privacy

CantonLink is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 12. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located outside your country. By using CantonLink, you consent to international data transfers.

## 13. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 14. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact:

**Email:** privacy@cantonlink.io
